About RAN ONLINE EP9

RAN ONLINE EP9 is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) where players can choose from various classes, engage in PvP battles, and explore a vast open world.

Classes

Brawler

Masters of close combat, Brawlers excel in hand-to-hand combat with devastating punches and kicks.

Swordsman

Wielders of powerful blades, Swordsmen are versatile warriors who dominate the battlefield.

Archer

Experts in ranged combat, Archers strike from a distance with precision and agility.

Shaman

Mystical spellcasters, Shamans harness the power of nature and spirits to support allies and defeat enemies.

Extreme

Unstoppable forces, Extreme fighters combine raw power and speed to overwhelm their foes.

Gunner

Masters of firearms, Gunners unleash rapid-fire attacks and explosive damage.
